The Essence of Parsi Heritage

The Parsis, whose name means "Persians", are descended from Persian Zoroastrians who emigrated to India to avoid religious persecution by the Muslims. They have a rich and unique cultural heritage deeply rooted in their Zoroastrian faith. Parsis have made significant contributions to various fields including education, business, arts, and philanthropy. Their distinct rituals, festivals, and traditions continue to be celebrated and passed down through generations.
