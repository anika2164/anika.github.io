Cuisine

Parsi cuisine is a blend of Iranian, Indian, and British influences. It is known for its unique flavors, aromatic spices, and rich use of ingredients such as meats, fish, vegetables, and lentils. Some popular Parsi dishes include Dhansak, Patra ni Machi, and Sali Boti.

Attire

Parsi men traditionally wear a white cotton kurta, which is a long tunic, paired with white cotton trousers. They also wear a traditional cap called "dagi," which is usually black or dark-colored. Parsi women often wear the traditional saree or the Parsi Gara embroidery saree, which is known for its intricate designs.
