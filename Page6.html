Reasons for Parsi Decline

There are several factors contributing to the decline in the Parsi population. These include low fertility rates, late marriages, emigration, and cultural assimilation. The small size of the Parsi community and the challenges in preserving their distinct identity have also played a role in the decline.

If you would like to learn more about Parsi decline, you can visit this website.
