These are 3 main sustainable practices followed by the Parsis:

Vulture Valor

Embracing the age-old Parsi tradition of Dokhmenashini, Vulture Valor is an eco-responsible approach to life's final journey. By allowing nature to play its part, it reduces land use and ensures the environment remains uncontaminated.

Parsi Preserves

Delving deep into Parsi heritage, Parsi Preserves embodies the community's dedication to recycling. By repurposing and reusing materials, this practice stands as a testament to sustainability, echoing the age-old ethos of reducing waste.

Aqua Reverence

Rooted in Parsi rituals that hold water sacred, Aqua Reverence champions the cause of water conservation. It's not just about mindful consumption but an ode to the deep-seated respect for nature's most vital resource.
